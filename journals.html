Journaling Ideas

"Journaling is one of the most powerful tools we have to transform our lives." - Deepak Chopra

Free Writing

This one is easy to begin with, just write down whatever comes to mind. If your mind has been feeling loud writing it all out can help you return to a sense of calm.

Highlight Journal

Each night write down just the best parts of the day.

Dream Journal

Writing down whatever you remember from your dreams can increase the amount of dreams you remember each night. It may even help with sleep quality.

Food Diary

Keeping a food diary can help you find which foods make you feel good and which ones are causing you problems.
